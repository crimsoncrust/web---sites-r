FAQ

Do I have to become a member with Accurate Credit Bureau to screen my potential tenants?

  • Yes. To obtain credit information, you must become a member.
  • We offer a few packages such as Criminal Reports, and Eviction records that do not require membership.
  • Membership is FREE and there are no set-up costs.
  • Click Here to sign up.

Can I email a credit check request?

  • Yes they can be emailed to info@accuratecredit.com

Where is Accurate Credit Bureau located?

  • We are located in Bastrop, TX, a small town outside of Austin.

Can you provide credit reports for applicants in any state?

  • Yes. Our services are nationwide, including Puerto Rico and the USVI.

What if my applicant does not have a Social Security Number? Can I still screen him/her?

  • We are able to provide Criminal Background Searches as well as Eviction Searches for applicants that do not have a Social Security Number.

Will I get the FULL consumer credit report?

  • FULL consumer credit reports can only be obtained after an On-Site Inspection.
  • This is mandated by law and is require by all background screening agencies.
  • ClickHere for more information about an on-site inspection.

What credit information will be provided on the credit check?

  • Bankruptcies
  • Collections
  • Judgements
  • Foreclosures
  • Total Number of Accounts
  • Total Number of Current Accounts
  • Applicant's FICO Score (with on-site inspection)
